About OpenSight

What is OpenSight?

OpenSight is a professional-grade analytics platform for Counter-Strike 2 teams. We provide deep insights into gameplay performance by analyzing demo files locally, ensuring your data stays private and secure.

Whether you're a casual team looking to improve or a competitive squad preparing for tournaments, OpenSight gives you the metrics you need to dominate.

Privacy First

Your demo files are processed on-demand and immediately deleted after analysis. We don't store your gameplay data or share it with third parties.

All analysis is done server-side with automatic cleanup

Metrics Explained

Time to Damage (TTD)

Measures reaction time from when you first see an enemy to when you deal damage. Lower values indicate faster reactions.

Crosshair Placement (CP)

Analyzes how close your crosshair is to enemy positions before engagements. Higher scores mean better pre-aim habits.

Trade Kills

Counts kills that happen within 2 seconds of a teammate dying to the same enemy. Shows team coordination and refrag ability.

Opening Duels

Tracks the first kill or death of each round. High opening kill percentage indicates strong entry fragging.

Economy Metrics

Analyzes weapon choices, money efficiency, and impact during eco/force rounds.

Overall Rating

A weighted score (0-100) combining K/D, crosshair placement, opening success, and trade effectiveness.
